About the Lab

The Tufts Robot Teaching Lab provides a dedicated space for students to explore robotics and work on human-robot interaction. We have multiple robots, including Turtlebots, Spheros, and Misty robots. Our goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and real-world applications.

Students engage in hands-on projects ranging from navigation and mapping to complex social interactions, preparing them for future research and industry roles in robotics.
